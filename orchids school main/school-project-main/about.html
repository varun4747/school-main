ABOUT ORCHIDS ENGLISH MEDIUM HIGH SCHOOL
ORCHIDS E.M. HIGH SCHOOL was established in 2006 and it is managed by the chairman. It is loacted in Addanki of Prakasam district in Andhra Pradesh. The school consists of grades from PRE-KG to 10th. The school is co-educational and it has an attached pre-primary section. The academic session of this school usually starts from June.
