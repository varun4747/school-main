Uniform Description

Our school uniform is designed to be both practical and smart. It helps create a sense of community and equality among students. Below are the guidelines for our uniform.

Guidelines

  • White and navy blue shirt with school logo
  • Navy blue trousers or skirt
  • Black shoes
  • Identity cards for each and every student
  • No jewelry except for stud earrings
  • Hair should be neatly tied back
